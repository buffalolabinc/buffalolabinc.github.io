As such, membership in the organization has a very low barrier to entry. And by low, we mean FREE! Yes, it always has and always will be free to become a Member of Buffalo Lab. As a Member you are given the following benefits:
  • Free admission to all activities listed on our event calendar
  • Internet access at the space
  • Materials, work space and other resources to work on projects
  • Satisfaction of being apart of a local growing community!
